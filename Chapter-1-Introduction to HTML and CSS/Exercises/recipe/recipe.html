Alfredo Chicken Pasta

This cozy Alfredo Chicken Pasta recipe is a perfect warm, cozy, decadent cheesy burst of flavour in your mouth that will definitely be a staple dish at your home for you and your family to enjoy!

INGREDIENTS

  • Unsalted butter (2 tablespoons)
  • 350g Penne/Pasta of your choice
    • |\
  • Skinless, boneless Chicken breast cubed (2)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 2 cups milk (480 ml)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a large pan over medium-high heat, then add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown. Season with the salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. .
  2. Over medium heat in the same pan, melt the butter and saute the garlic. Saute until the garlic begins to soften.
  3. Add half the flour at a time, stirring until dissolved. Add the milk and heavy cream gradually, stirring between additions, and cook until absorbed and the sauce begins to thicken.
  4. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss well to coat evenly.
  5. Let it simmer for a few minutes until thickened slightly. Garnish with fresh parsley.
  6. Serve hot with extra cheese on top if desired!
